'''Ryan A. Costello''' (born September 7, 1976) is an American lawyer and politician from the state of [[Pennsylvania]]. Since 2011, he has served on the [[Chester County, Pennsylvania|Chester County]] [[County commission|Board of Commissioners]], and as Chairman since 2013. A [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]], Costello is the representative-elect for {{ushr|PA|6}} in the [[United States House of Representatives elections in Pennsylvania, 2014|2014 elections]] to the [[United States House of Representatives]].

==Early life==
Costello was born in 1976 to schoolteacher parents.<ref>[http://www.nationaljournal.com/article/534408 Profile], nationaljournal.com; accessed November 9, 2014.</ref> Costello attended [[Ursinus College]] and [[Villanova University School of Law]].<ref name=th>{{cite web|url=http://www.timesherald.com/general-news/20140115/chester-county-commissioner-ryan-costello-to-run-for-jim-gerlachs-seat|title=Chester County Commissioner Ryan Costello to run for Jim Gerlach's seat|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

==Political career==
Costello served on the [[Board of Supervisors]] for [[East Vincent Township, Chester County, Pennsylvania|East Vincent Township]] in [[Chester County, Pennsylvania]], for six years, serving as chairman for the last four. He was elected the Chester County [[recorder of deeds]] in 2008.<ref name=th/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.unionvilletimes.com/?p=1684|title=Costello appointed Chester County Commissioner|work=The Unionville Times|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref> He was elected to the Chester County [[County commission|Board of Commissioners]] in 2011.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.philly.com/philly/blogs/capitolinq/Chester-Republican-Costello-announces-run-for-Congress.html|title=Chester County Republican Costello announces run for Congress|work=Philly.com|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref> His fellow commissioners elected him as Chairman of the commission in 2013, and reappointed in 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.chestercounty.com/2014/01/14/20238/costello-re-appointed-chair-of-chester-county-commissioners|title=Costello re-appointed chair of Chester County Commissioners|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref>

When [[Jim Gerlach]], the [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] incumbent in {{ushr|PA|6}} in the [[United States House of Representatives]], announced that he would not run for reelection in 2014, Costello chose to run for the Republican Party nomination.<ref name=star>{{cite web|url=http://www.chestercounty.com/2014/05/14/41418/the-republican-partys-rising-star|title=The Republican Party's rising star|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref> He faced no primary opposition.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wfmz.com/news/news-regional-berks/Local/6th-district-candidates-unopposed-in-primary-look-to-november-election/26078572|title=6th District candidates unopposed in primary, look to November election|author=WFMZ|work=WFMZ|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref> 

He faced [[Manan Trivedi]] of the [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic Party]] in the general election.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://readingeagle.com/news/article/in-battle-for-congress-ryan-costello-and-manan-trivedi-vie-for-money-and-attention|title=In battle for Congress, Ryan Costello and Manan Trivedi vie for money and attention|work=Reading Eagle|accessdate=November 4, 2014}}</ref> Costello defeated Trivedi, 56%-44%.<ref>[http://www.wfmz.com/news/news-regional-berks/republican-ryan-costello-beats-manan-trivedi-for-6th-district-us-house-seat/29536542 Ryan Costello beats Manan Trivedi for 6th U.S. House Congressional seat], wfmz.com; accessed November 9, 2014.</ref>

==Personal life==
Ryan Costello and his wife and son live in [[West Chester, Pennsylvania]].<ref name=th/>
