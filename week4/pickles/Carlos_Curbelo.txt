'''Carlos Curbelo''' (born March 1, 1980) is an American politician who was elected to serve [[Florida's 26th congressional district]] in the [[United States House of Representatives elections, 2014]]. He will take office on January 3, 2015.

==Career==
Curbelo is a member of the [[Miami-Dade County Public Schools]] board.<ref name="MiamiHerldMazzei11052014">Mazzei, Patricia, Christina Veiga, and Daniel Chang. [http://www.miamiherald.com/news/local/community/miami-dade/article3569095.html In GOP pickup, Miami Rep. Joe Garcia loses to Carlos Curbelo], ''[[Miami Herald]]'', November 5, 2014.</ref> He attended the [[University of Miami]] where he earned a bachelor's and masters degree in [[public administration]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://district7.dadeschools.net/biography.html|title=Carlos Curbelo - District 7 School Board|publisher=|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref> He is the founder of Capitol Gains, a government and public relations firm.<ref name="votesmart">{{cite web|url=http://votesmart.org/candidate/biography/137676/carlos-curbelo#.VFk72_TF_Zk|title=Project Vote Smart - The Voter's Self Defense System|work=Project Vote Smart|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://dailyentertainmentnews.com/politics/cecilia-curbelo-miami-congressional-candidate-carlos-curbelos-wife/|title=Cecilia Curbelo: Miami congressional candidate Carlos Curbelo's Wife (bio, wiki, photos)|work=DailyEntertainmentNews.com|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref>

He is also a former state director for former U.S. Senator [[George LeMieux]] of Florida.<ref name="MiamiHerldMazzei11052014"/>

In the [[United States House of Representatives elections in Florida, 2014#District 26|2014 election]], Curbelo defeated [[incumbent]] [[Joe Garcia]] of the [[Democratic Party (United States)|Democratic Party]] by 52 to 48 percent.<ref name="MiamiHerldMazzei11052014"/><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.local10.com/news/fight-for-district-26-heats-up-at-polls-in-southwest-miamidade/29527752|title=Carlos Curbelo defeats Joe Garcia in fight for District 26|author=WPLG|work=Local10|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Curbelo is the son of [[Cuban]] exiles in Florida.<ref>Werner, Erica. [http://hosted.ap.org/dynamic/stories/U/US_NEW_HOUSE_REPUBLICANS?SITE=AP&SECTION=HOME&TEMPLATE=DEFAULT&CTIME=2014-11-08-09-17-42 House GOP boasts diversity and new conservatives], ''[[Associated Press]]'', November 8, 2014</ref> He married Cecilia Lowell, sister of former [[Miami Marlins|Marlins]] third baseman [[Mike Lowell]] in 2006 and resides in [[Kendall, Florida]].<ref name="votesmart" />
