'''John Michael Katko''' (born November 9, 1962) is an American politician who was elected to serve [[New York's 24th congressional district|New York's 24th district]] in the [[United States House of Representatives elections, 2014]]. He will take office on January 3, 2015.

==Career==
Katko is a former federal prosecutor. He attended [[Niagara University]] where he earned a bachelor of arts degree in political science, and the [[Syracuse University College of Law]] where he earned a [[juris doctorate]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://votesmart.org/candidate/biography/152546/john-katko#.VFmu2_TF_Zk|title=Project Vote Smart - The Voter's Self Defense System|work=Project Vote Smart|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref>

He ran against [[Dan Maffei]] in the [[United States House of Representatives elections, 2014]] and was declared the winner on November 4, 2014 by 20 percentage points--the largest margin of defeat suffered by an incumbent in the cycle.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.syracuse.com/news/index.ssf/2014/11/john_katko_declared_winner_over_rep_dan_maffei_in_race_for_congress.html|title=John Katko declared winner over Rep. Dan Maffei in race for Congress|work=syracuse.com|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Katko was born in Syracuse in 1962, and is a graduate of [[Bishop Ludden Junior/Senior High School|Bishop Ludden High School]].<ref>[http://www.nationaljournal.com/article/535254 Profile], nationaljournal.com; accessed November 10, 2014.</ref> 

Katko was raised in suburban [[Camillus, New York]], where he now resides with his wife, Robin Katko and their sons: Sean, Liam & Logan.<ref name=meet>{{cite web|url=http://www.syracuse.com/news/index.ssf/2014/10/john_katko_gang_prosecutor_thrives_on_chaos_competition_wants_to_go_to_congress.html|title=Meet John Katko: Career gang prosecutor talks of taking on 'knuckleheads' in Congress|work=syracuse.com|accessdate=November 5, 2014}}</ref>
