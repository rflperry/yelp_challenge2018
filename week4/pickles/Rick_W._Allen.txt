'''Richard W. Allen''' (born November 7, 1951) is an American politician who was elected to serve [[Georgia's 12th congressional district]] in the [[United States House of Representatives elections, 2014]]. He will take office on January 3, 2015.

==Career==
Allen attended [[Auburn University]] and earned a degree in building construction. He is the founder of R.W. Allen and Associates, a construction company headquartered in Augusta.<ref>http://votesmart.org/candidate/biography/136062/rick-allen#.VFlwdfTF_Zk</ref>

Allen ran against [[John Barrow (U.S. politician)|John Barrow]] in the [[United States House of Representatives elections in Georgia, 2014|2014 election]] for {{ushr|GA|12}} in the [[United States House of Representatives]]. Allen defeated Barrow in the November election, in a result considered an upset.<ref>http://politics.blog.ajc.com/2014/11/04/tonights-rolling-blog-cnn-exit-polls-put-georgia-u-s-senate-race-at-49-48/</ref><ref>http://www.ajc.com/news/news/state-regional-govt-politics/rick-allen-upsets-john-barrow-for-georgia/nhzbx/</ref>

==Personal life==
Allen lives in [[Augusta, Georgia]]. A [[Methodist]], he is married to Robin and has four children.
